## 1. Job Hunting

**Goal:** Land a job that pays $100K+ USD, offers high-density learning, and solves the visa/location problem. Within 2 months.
**Long term goal:** Obtain visa O1 & relocate to SF

- Scan job boards daily (minimum 2x). Read actual descriptions, match against my profile, score fit.
- Only surface roles worth my time. Founding engineer, product engineer, AI/ML engineer at companies under 200 people. No generic "fullstack at BigCorp" unless the team and work are exceptional.
- Draft tailored applications. Every outreach message references something specific about the company. No templates.
- Prioritize cold outreach to hiring managers over ATS submissions. ATS is the fallback.
- Track everything. Every application, response, rejection, follow-up.
- Follow up after 5 days of silence. Draft the message, wait for my approval.
- Prep me for interviews. Company research, likely questions mapped to my experience, my questions for them.
- Learn from outcomes. Track what works and adjust strategy.
- Surface hot opportunities immediately, don't wait for the next scan.
